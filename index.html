  • The Hypocrites

    Story LineThe Igala belief system holds it a crime when two brothers have sexual relations with the same woman. This and adultery are some of the taboos (Amenwu elifo) that provoke the wrath of the ancestral spirits (Abegwu) whose retribution manifests in varying physical an non physical afflictions, often endig in death, if of course the necessary cleansing rites are not performed. In the movie, the precepts of the "African God" as presented by the Igala cosmology in its wholistic nature is juxtaposed with those of the "Western God" as upheld by the Christian faithful. One is then forced to conclude that, if it was God who created the cosmos, and that same God controls the path of man, then, that God is universal.
